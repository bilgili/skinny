## What Changes

- **Gate BDPT's `t = 0` emissive eye-vertex hit** exactly like the path tracer.
  Add `z.throughput * z.emission` at full weight only when no NEE partner exists:
  the primary/first hit (`s == 2`, the analog of `path.slang`'s `bounce == 0`),
  a delta/specular bounce into the light (`eye[s - 2].isDelta`), or a scene with
  no emissive-triangle NEE (`fc.numEmissiveTriangles == 0`). Otherwise `connectT1`
  (the `t = 1` NEE) owns the area light, matching the path tracer.
- **Apply to both BDPT surfaces**: the megakernel (`bdpt.slang`) and the wavefront
  shade stage (`wavefront_bdpt.slang`) â€” they share `connectT1` / `misWeight` and
  carried the identical unweighted loop.
- **Add a BDPT-vs-pbrt absolute-energy gate** (`tests/pbrt/test_bdpt_energy.py`):
  the existing parity / convergence gates exposure-align before comparing, which
  divides out a uniform brightness error, so they cannot see a 1.7Ã— scale. The new
  gate compares *un-aligned* mean energy on `diffuse_arealight` and `glass_arealight`
  (megakernel + wavefront), asserting `mean(bdpt)/mean(ref)` within tolerance and
  `mean(bdpt)/mean(path)` â‰ˆ 1.
